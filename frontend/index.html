      About JobLens

      A transparent, fair, and explainable AI-powered interview platform built for the modern recruitment era.

      Our Mission

      JobLens was born from a simple observation: traditional interviews are time-consuming, biased, and inconsistent. We set out to build a better way — one that uses AI to evaluate candidates fairly, transparently, and at scale.

      Unlike opaque AI systems that rely on facial analysis or emotion detection, JobLens evaluates only what matters: what candidates say, how clearly they say it, and how well they solve technical problems.

      Why JobLens?

      🎯
      Rubric-Driven

      Every score is backed by explicit rubrics — no black boxes, full explainability.

      ⚖️
      Bias-Free

      No video, no emotion analysis. Only content and communication quality are evaluated.

      📈
      Scalable

      Handle unlimited concurrent interviews — perfect for high-volume hiring.
